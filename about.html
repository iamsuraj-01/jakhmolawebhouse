About Us

Who we are, what we do, and why we do it.

Our Journey

Jakhmola Web House was founded in 2014 in the beautiful town of Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, with a mission to empower startups, entrepreneurs, and service providers to thrive in the digital landscape. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include web development, digital marketing, branding, social media management, and more. Our passion is to drive growth through digital excellence, both locally and globally.

Meet Our Team

Our Mission

To build a vibrant digital community by connecting local ambitions with global opportunities, and to innovate with quality in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To be a leading digital partner for businesses worldwide, creating impact and value through excellence, creativity, and dedication.
