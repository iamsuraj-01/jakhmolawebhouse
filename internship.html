Eligibility Criteria

  • 1. Students and Recent Graduates: Individuals currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a degree program related to marketing, business, communications, or a similar field.
  • 2. Aspiring Digital Marketers: Those who are passionate about digital marketing.
  • 3. Local Residents: Who are looking to enhance their skills and gain practical experience in digital marketing.
  • 4. Career Switchers: Professionals from other industries who are looking to transition into digital marketing.
  • 5. Self-taught Individuals: Those who have independently gained practical experience.
  • 6. Entrepreneurs and Small Business Owners: Individuals who want to learn digital marketing to better promote their own businesses.
