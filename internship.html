Internship Program

Jakhmola Web House has collaborated with local universities to offer a digital marketing internship program to local students. This initiative aims to equip them with digital marketing techniques, enabling them to advance in their careers and contribute to the growth of their services.

Collaborated with Sri Dev Suman Rishikesh University and Omkaranand Institute of Management to provide students in Rishikesh with digital knowledge. During the internships, students are taught how to improve their startups, enhance their services, and increase job opportunities through digital marketing. Interns will learn essential SEO techniques, social media marketing, branding, and website management.

The program not only focuses on digital marketing skills but also provides hands-on experience with real-world projects. By aligning with industry standards, we ensure that students are well-prepared to enter the job market and excel in their careers. Additionally, students gain valuable networking opportunities with industry professionals, which enhances their employment prospects. Upon successful completion of the internship, they receive a certification that adds value to their resumes. Regular mentorship sessions are held to guide interns through challenges, ensuring personal and professional growth. Finally, the exposure to diverse digital platforms enables students to build a versatile skill set.
